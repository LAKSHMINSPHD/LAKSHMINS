About

Dr. Lakshmi N S is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Kottayam, Kerala. She earned her B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering and M.Tech in VLSI and Embedded Systems from MG University, Kerala, and subsequently completed her Ph.D. at the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli (NIT Trichy) in 2022. Her doctoral research focused on Gyrator-C based active inductors and their application in bandpass filter and VCO design for reconfigurable RF front-ends. Prior to joining IIIT Kottayam, she held teaching positions at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (Amritapuri Campus) and the Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT Vellore), where she taught both undergraduate and postgraduate courses.
